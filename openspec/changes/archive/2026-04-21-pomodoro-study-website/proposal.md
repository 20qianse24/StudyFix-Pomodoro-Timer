## Why

The goal is to provide a clean, minimalist Pomodoro study tool that helps users maintain focus through structured study intervals. By providing a distraction-free web interface with preset intervals and a modern aesthetic, users can more effectively manage their time and productivity.

## What Changes

- Creation of a minimalist web application with a centered layout and soft color palette.
- Implementation of a digital MM:SS timer with Start, Pause, and Reset functionality.
- Addition of preset interval buttons for 30 minutes, 1 hour, and 1.5 hours.
- Visual highlighting of the active preset button.
- Integration of a subtle audio notification when the timer completes.
- Structural preparation of the DOM for future OpenAI playlist generation and task checklist features.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- `pomodoro-timer`: Core timer logic, digital display, presets (30m, 1h, 1.5h), and notification sound.
- `study-interface`: Minimalist UI design, centered layout, and visual state management for active presets.
- `task-checklist-placeholder`: Structural DOM section for future task management.
- `music-player-placeholder`: Structural DOM section for future music playlist features.
